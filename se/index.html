How we want to help
Ninaada foundation has always been about tackling problems at the lowest level. Face to Face fundraising is one of the key fundraising initiatives for Save the Children. Face to Face fundraisers are 'our Face' on the street. We sincerely thank and are immensely proud of our fundraisers who engage in Door-to-door, street/outdoor fundraising and other forms of Face to Face fundraising. They bring in a significant amount of donations for us, which are then utilized for the work Save the Children does, which included running programmes on Health & Nutrition, Child Protection, Education and Humanitarian work.
